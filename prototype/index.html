How It Changes Your Plan

Decision logic
If you feel stronger

Keep the current split, consider small load increases, and preserve the exercises that are clearly working.

If you feel the same

Check weekly volume, rep progression, and exercise redundancy before making a moderate tweak.

If you feel weaker

Look for recovery issues, excessive overlap, low calories, or repeated regressions before cutting load or volume.
